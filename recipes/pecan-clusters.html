Pecan Clusters

Description

Chocolate covered pecans. Good for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Cocolate Chips
  • 2 Cups Pecan Halves
  • Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Melt chocolate chips in a sauce pan.
  3. Once chocolate chips are fully melted, dump in pecan halves and mix thoroughly.
  4. Spoon out "clusters" onto cookie sheet
  5. Sprinkle with sea salt
  6. Freeze for one hour
  7. Enjoy!
